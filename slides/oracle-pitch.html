The problem

Half your leads go dark — and you can't tell
which ones are coming back.

A solar rep opens Monday to 40 open quotes. Each one cost real money to acquire. Then comes the silence: a lead stops replying. Price? Trust? Financing? Already signed with someone else?

  • ~48% of reps never follow up even once. And ~80% of deals need 5+ touches.
  • The clock is brutal: qualify-odds drop ~21× if you wait 30 min instead of 5 (MIT / InsideSales Lead-Response study).
  • So attention goes to the wrong leads — reps chase dead quotes while the ones one message from signing quietly slip away.
Problem01 / 05
The solution

Two honest numbers for every lead —
sign probability and ghost risk.

Plus a confidence band, the one dominant blocker, a single recommended action, and the evidence behind it.

Aha #1
Numbers from a model, words from the LLM. A statistical survival model owns the probabilities; the LLM only explains them. It never invents a percentage.
Aha #2
Ghost risk assumes you do nothing. The recommended action — which resets the silence clock — is the actual lever that moves the number. We hand you the one move that changes the outcome.
Aha #3
It tells the truth about itself. An honest "Uncalibrated" badge until real outcomes accrue — no faked confidence.
